// about

Qualified. Practical. Doesn't muck around.

Ashley has been in the trade for over a decade — workshop floors, mine sites, race paddocks, and 4am breakdowns on the side of the highway. He diagnoses first, talks second, and charges honestly.

He treats every vehicle like it's his own: clean terminations, correct fusing, sensible routing. No bodged repairs, no "she'll be right," no surprise invoices.

He's also the one you want on remote jobs. Self-sufficient, organised, used to working in tough conditions with whatever's on hand. Toolbox, laptop, and a head full of wiring diagrams.
